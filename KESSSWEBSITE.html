Welcome to KESSS

We are delighted to welcome you to the official online home of Kanifing East Senior Secondary School. Our institution is dedicated to fostering a rich environment for academic excellence, character development, and civic responsibility. We believe in nurturing the potential of every student to become a confident and successful leader of tomorrow.

Explore our programs, meet our dedicated faculty, and discover why KESSS is the premier choice for senior secondary education in the region. We are committed to maintaining high standards of discipline and learning, ensuring a brighter future for all our graduates.

Our Academic Excellence

At Kanifing East Senior Secondary School, our curriculum is designed to provide students with the skills, knowledge, and confidence they need to excel in a rapidly evolving world.

Academic Streams

We offer diverse streams to prepare students for higher education and professional life:

  • Science Stream: Engineering, Medicine, Research.
  • Commerce Stream: Business, Accounting, Finance.
  • Art Stream: Literature, History, Critical Thinking.
